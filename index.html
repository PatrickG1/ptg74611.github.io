University Golf Course

The University Golf Course is as beautiful to look at as it is challenging to play. Set on the rolling terrain of northeast Georgia, this par 71 Robert Trent Jones layout is widely regarded as one of the best university courses in the United States. Renovated by Love Golf Design in 2006, the course in now up to the standards of modern championship play and is home to both of the Universityâ€™s top ranked golf teams. While extremely challenging from the championship Bulldog Tees, the course has four other sets of tees to help golfers of all abilities enjoy their round on this classic layout.

The University Course at UGA is a nice but challenging golf course located just off campus in Athens. It is celebrating its 50 year anniversary this year.

2017-2018 Schedule

The first matches this year, excluding the fall, was on February 18th in Rio Grande, Puerto Rico. Our best finish so far was 2nd, where we shot 3 under par as a team. The first rounds of the Southern Intercollegiates were March 12. We played in Athens at the Athens Country Club. This tournament is still being played. The SEC Championship tournament begins April 25th and will be played all week in St. Simons Island, GA at the Seaside Golf Resort. The NCAA National Championship Tournament begins May 25th.
